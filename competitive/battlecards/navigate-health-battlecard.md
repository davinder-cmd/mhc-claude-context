## Objection Handling

### "They have pharmacist-led coaching for chronic conditions"
> **Response:** "Pharmacist coaching is valuable for medication management, but it's not comprehensive condition management. Our Digital Care Paths combine clinical protocols with behavior change science - addressing the full spectrum of what drives health improvement. And we guarantee outcomes."

### "They have 93% client retention"
> **Response:** "Retention is important - satisfied clients stay. But retention doesn't equal outcomes. Our ROI guarantee means we're committed to delivering measurable results, not just keeping clients comfortable. We guarantee 3:1 ROI or return 30% of fees."

### "Their G2 reviews are excellent - 4.9 stars"
> **Response:** "Good reviews are great, and we're glad their clients like them. But G2 reviews measure user experience, not clinical outcomes or cost savings. We focus on what matters to your bottom line - and we guarantee it."

### "They've been around for 17 years"
> **Response:** "Longevity can mean experience, or it can mean legacy approaches. Our platform is built for modern engagement and clinical outcomes. We deliver Digital Care Paths that provide measurable ROI - and we guarantee it."

### "They just redesigned their platform in 2025"
> **Response:** "Platform refreshes are nice, but the question is what the platform delivers. Does a new UI reduce healthcare costs? Our focus is on outcomes - clinical programs that treat conditions and guarantee ROI."
