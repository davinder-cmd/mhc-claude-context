## Landmine Questions

Ask prospects to ask Personify:

1. **"What's your clinical protocol for [specific condition]?"**
   *Exposes: Their programs may be generic vs. our validated DCPs*

2. **"Do you guarantee ROI? What happens if outcomes aren't achieved?"**
   *Exposes: No guarantee vs. our 3:1 commitment*

3. **"Can we buy wellbeing without TPA services?"**
   *Exposes: Bundle complexity and potential over-buying*

4. **"What are your actual engagement rates on clinical programs?"**
   *Exposes: May have high app downloads but low clinical engagement*

5. **"How do you handle employees who need specialized point solutions?"**
   *Exposes: Platform may not go deep enough*

## Objection Handling

### "Personify is bigger and has more clients"
> **Response:** "Scale doesn't equal outcomes. Our Digital Care Paths deliver measurable clinical results - and we guarantee a 3:1 ROI or return 30% of fees. They don't offer that. Would you rather have a big platform or proven outcomes?"

### "They're the only one with TPA and wellbeing together"
> **Response:** "That's true - and it's also their weakness. Bundling creates complexity. You may not need TPA services, but you'll pay for them. Our focused platform integrates with your existing benefits ecosystem without the overhead."

### "Virgin Pulse is a known brand - we've heard of them"
> **Response:** "Brand recognition doesn't reduce claims costs. What matters is whether your employees actually improve their health. Let me show you our outcomes data - and remember, we guarantee results."

### "We want one vendor to simplify things"
> **Response:** "We understand the appeal of consolidation - that's exactly what we help with. We replace multiple point solutions (MSK, behavioral, diabetes) with one platform. The difference is we do it without forcing you into TPA services you may not need."
