## Objection Handling

### "Everyone knows RealAge - it's a trusted brand"
> **Response:** "Brand awareness doesn't equal engagement or outcomes. Have you seen their app reviews? Their NPS is -7 - meaning more detractors than promoters. Our engagement rates and clinical outcomes speak for themselves. And we guarantee ROI."

### "They have NCQA accreditation for chronic conditions"
> **Response:** "NCQA accreditation is a minimum standard, not a differentiator. It means they meet baseline requirements - it doesn't mean their programs actually change behavior or reduce costs. Our DCPs are designed for outcomes, backed by our ROI guarantee."

### "They won 22 Digital Health Awards"
> **Response:** "Awards recognize submissions and marketing, not member outcomes. Ask them for engagement rates and clinical results data. We're happy to put our outcomes side-by-side with anyone."

### "They have the AR Wellness Lens - very innovative"
> **Response:** "AR is interesting technology, but does it reduce your healthcare costs? A 60-second AR experience doesn't treat diabetes or chronic back pain. Our programs address the conditions driving your claims spend."
