## Landmine Questions

Ask prospects to ask Thrive:

1. **"How do you handle employees with diabetes, MSK issues, or serious mental health needs?"**
   *Exposes: Limited clinical depth for chronic conditions*

2. **"How do you measure ROI? What metrics do you guarantee?"**
   *Exposes: They measure "health actions" not clinical outcomes*

3. **"What happens after the 60-second Resets? Is there clinical follow-through?"**
   *Exposes: Content vs. clinical programs gap*

4. **"Do we still need point solutions for specific conditions?"**
   *Exposes: They can't replace clinical point solutions*

5. **"Is the AI coach providing clinical guidance or motivational content?"**
   *Exposes: Motivation vs. treatment difference*

## Objection Handling

### "Arianna Huffington started this - it's a credible brand"
> **Response:** "Celebrity founders get headlines, but do they reduce healthcare costs? We focus on clinical outcomes and guarantee ROI - 3:1 or we return 30% of fees. Can they say the same?"

### "They have an OpenAI partnership - cutting-edge AI"
> **Response:** "AI is only as good as what it delivers. Their AI coach provides motivational nudges. Our Digital Care Paths deliver clinically validated programs that treat conditions and reduce claims. We measure outcomes, not buzzwords."

### "Our employees love Microsteps and the 60-second Resets"
> **Response:** "Engagement is great, but engagement doing what? A 60-second reset in Slack doesn't treat diabetes or chronic back pain. Our programs address the conditions driving your healthcare spend - and we guarantee the results."

### "They integrate with Slack and Teams - so convenient"
> **Response:** "Convenience is nice, but convenience doesn't equal outcomes. We can integrate with your workflow too - and we deliver clinical programs that actually improve health, not just daily reminders."

### "Microsoft uses them"
> **Response:** "That's a great logo for them. But Microsoft has different needs than most employers - and unlimited resources for multiple solutions. What matters for you is outcomes and ROI. Let's talk about what we can guarantee."
