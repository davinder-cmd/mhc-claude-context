## Summary: Mobile Health Competitive Advantages

### Where Mobile Health Leads
1. **DCP Clinical Depth** - Comprehensive programs for Behavioral Health, Diabetes, MSK, Heart, Weight, Pregnancy
2. **ROI Guarantee** - 3:1 ROI or 30% fees returned (strongest in market)
3. **Partner Distribution** - Embedded in Alight, Elevance, Lockton, WTW, Aon ecosystems
4. **SDK/White-label** - Unique ability to embed in third-party apps
5. **Consolidation Story** - Replace point solutions without TPA complexity
6. **Pricing** - More affordable than enterprise-focused competitors

### Where Mobile Health May Trail
1. **Scale/Brand** - Smaller than Personify Health (25M) or Sharecare (75M)
2. **AI/Tech Marketing** - Less buzz than Thrive's OpenAI partnership
3. **TPA Integration** - No health plan admin capabilities (Personify differentiator)
4. **Enterprise Logos** - May have fewer Fortune 500 references than Personify
