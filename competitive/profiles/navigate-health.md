## 10. Win Themes vs. Navigate Wellbeing

### When Mobile Health Wins
1. **ROI Guarantee:** 3:1 ROI or 30% fees returned - they don't offer this
2. **Clinical Depth:** DCPs are more comprehensive than pharmacist coaching alone
3. **Partner Distribution:** Elevance, Lockton, WTW, Aon relationships
4. **Digital Therapeutics Focus:** Purpose-built for clinical outcomes
5. **Scale:** Larger client base and member reach

### Objection Handling

**"They have pharmacist-led coaching for chronic conditions"**
> "Pharmacist coaching is valuable for medication management, but comprehensive condition management requires more. Our DCPs combine clinical protocols with behavior change science - not just medication guidance. And we guarantee outcomes."

**"They have 93% client retention"**
> "Retention is important, and we take pride in ours too. But retention doesn't equal outcomes. Our ROI guarantee means we're committed to delivering measurable results, not just keeping clients comfortable."

**"Their G2 reviews are excellent (4.9 stars)"**
> "Good reviews matter, and we're glad their clients are satisfied. But those reviews measure user experience, not clinical outcomes or cost savings. We focus on what matters to your bottom line - and guarantee it."

**"They've been around for 17+ years"**
> "Longevity can mean experience, or it can mean legacy technology. Our platform is built for modern engagement and clinical outcomes. And while they've been around, we've been innovating - with Digital Care Paths that deliver measurable ROI."
