## 5. Pricing

### Model
- **PEPM (Per Employee Per Month)** pricing
- Customized based on population size and selected features
- Bundled solutions available (wellbeing + navigation + benefits admin)

### Estimated Range
- Pricing varies significantly based on scope
- TPA services: Higher PEPM ($400-700+ range for full admin)
- Wellbeing-only: Lower PEPM (estimated $3-8 range)
- Contact required for quotes

## 7. Weaknesses (Potential Gaps)

### From G2 Reviews (4.3/5 stars, 100+ reviews)
| Issue | Details |
|-------|---------|
| Technical Issues | Linking problems with tracking equipment; sleep data sync issues |
| App Stability | Users logged out after updates; trouble getting back in |
| Generic Content | "Most content is somewhat generic" - less personalized than claimed |
| Integration Challenges | Some users struggle connecting wearables |

### Strategic Weaknesses
1. **Post-Merger Integration:** Large merger may create integration challenges
2. **Complexity:** Being everything to everyone may mean not best-in-class anywhere
3. **Enterprise Focus:** May be overkill for mid-market employers
4. **Pricing Complexity:** Bundled model may be confusing for buyers
5. **Digital Therapeutics Depth:** May not match clinical depth of specialized DTx players

## 9. Landmine Questions (For Sales to Ask Prospects)

1. "How do you plan to handle the integration complexity of bundling TPA with wellbeing?"
2. "What's your actual engagement rate on clinical programs vs. general wellness features?"
3. "Can you share outcomes data specific to [condition] - not just aggregate savings?"
4. "How personalized is the content really - is it rules-based or truly adaptive AI?"
5. "What happens when employees need specialized DTx programs beyond your platform?"

## 10. Win Themes vs. Personify Health

### When Mobile Health Wins
1. **Clinical Depth:** Our DCPs are clinically rigorous, not generic content
2. **Price/Value:** Lower cost, focused solution vs. complex bundle
3. **Partner Relationships:** Elevance, Lockton, WTW, Aon distribution
4. **ROI Guarantee:** 3:1 ROI or 30% fees returned
5. **Consolidation Without Complexity:** Replace point solutions without TPA complexity

### Objection Handling

**"Personify is bigger and more comprehensive"**
> "Size doesn't equal outcomes. Our clinical DCPs deliver measurable results on specific conditions. We focus on what drives health improvement, not bundling services you may not need. Plus, our ROI guarantee puts our money where our mouth is."

**"We want one vendor for everything"**
> "We understand the appeal of consolidation, but bundling TPA with wellbeing often means compromises. Our focused platform integrates seamlessly with your existing benefits ecosystem while delivering deeper clinical outcomes. And you get choice - not vendor lock-in."
