## 7. Weaknesses (Potential Gaps)

| Source | Issue |
|--------|-------|

2. **Customer Service:** Complaints about responsiveness and support

## 10. Win Themes vs. Sharecare

### When Mobile Health Wins
1. **Clinical Depth:** Our DCPs go deeper than NCQA-minimum programs
2. **ROI Guarantee:** 3:1 ROI or 30% fees returned - they don't offer this
3. **User Experience:** Higher engagement, better app experience
4. **Support Quality:** Dedicated customer success, not call center hold times
5. **Focused Platform:** Purpose-built for employer wellbeing, not fragmented

### Objection Handling

**"Sharecare has RealAge - everyone knows that brand"**
> "Brand awareness doesn't equal engagement or outcomes. What matters is whether employees actually use the platform and improve their health. Our engagement rates and clinical outcomes speak for themselves. And we guarantee ROI - they don't."

**"They have NCQA accreditation for chronic conditions"**
> "NCQA accreditation is a baseline standard, not a differentiator. Our Digital Care Paths are designed for clinical outcomes, not just checkbox compliance. We focus on behavior change that sticks, backed by our ROI guarantee."

**"They won 22 Digital Health Awards"**
> "Awards recognize marketing and submissions, not member outcomes. Ask them for engagement rates and clinical outcomes data - then compare to ours. We're happy to put our results side-by-side."
