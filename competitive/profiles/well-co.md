## 10. Win Themes vs. Well.co

### When Mobile Health Wins
1. **Stronger ROI Guarantee:** 3:1 ROI vs. their 1:1 guarantee
2. **Clinical Depth:** Comprehensive DCPs vs. general wellness
3. **Established Track Record:** More clients, more proof points
4. **Partner Distribution:** Elevance, Lockton, WTW, Aon relationships
5. **Specific Condition Programs:** Diabetes, MSK, behavioral health depth

### Objection Handling

**"They also have an ROI guarantee"**
> "We both believe in guaranteeing results - that's great. But look at the guarantee: they promise 1:1, we promise 3:1 or 30% of fees returned. Our confidence in our clinical outcomes is three times theirs."

**"They have human coaches (Well Guides)"**
> "Human touch matters, and we agree. Our programs combine digital therapeutics with coaching where it's clinically appropriate. The difference is our DCPs are clinically validated for specific conditions - diabetes, MSK, behavioral health - not just general wellness guidance."

**"Their engagement metrics are high"**
> "Engagement is important, but engagement doing what? 300 interactions per year means nothing if they're not addressing the conditions driving your healthcare costs. Our DCPs engage members in clinically rigorous programs that deliver measurable outcomes."
