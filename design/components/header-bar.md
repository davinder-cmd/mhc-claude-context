## Purpose

Persistent top-of-screen surface for employer branding and top-level utility actions (profile, settings, messages). Present on all screens. Consistent across platforms.

---

## Known Usage

- Always present — cannot be removed or hidden
- Contains employer logo/branding
- On web: may include navigation or utility actions (profile, notifications)
- Must accommodate partner/employer brand customization within defined constraints
