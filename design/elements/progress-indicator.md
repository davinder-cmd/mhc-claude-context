## Purpose

A visual representation of a user's progress through a defined set of steps, guiding toward completion of a process.

---

## When to Use

- Multi-step flows (onboarding, program enrollment, surveys)
- When users benefit from knowing how far along they are and how much remains

## When NOT to Use

- Indeterminate loading states — use Loading Indicator instead
