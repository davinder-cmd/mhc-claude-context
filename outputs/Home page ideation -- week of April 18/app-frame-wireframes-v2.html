Content parity philosophy:
  • Mobile users deserve the same information density
  • Scrolling is natural — don't artificially truncate
  • Compact cards, not hidden content
  • All 8 modules visible without "load more"
Scroll depth: Home page is ~4-5 screens of scrolling on mobile. This is intentional — users scan what's relevant, scroll past what isn't. Key modules (Stats, Actions, AI Insights) are above the fold.
