Home Screen — Layout Wireframes

5 layout variants exploring section order, rewards presentation (4 reward types: gift cards, points, store currency, raffles), and content priority. All use Personify's design patterns: bold section headers with chevrons, generous inter-section spacing, single hero moment, progressive disclosure.
