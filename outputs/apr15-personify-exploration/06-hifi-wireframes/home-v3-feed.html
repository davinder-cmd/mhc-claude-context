Design Decisions:
• Stories row replaces fixed section headers. Each icon = category quick-link (48px circles).
• Status bar is a bare text ribbon, not a card—saves vertical space.
• Feed cards have 12px gap, chronologically ordered.
• Each card tagged (top-left) with type: "2h ago", "Action", "Challenge", "Just for you".
• No fixed sections. Content mixed by recency + engagement.
• Margins: 14px. Card padding: 10–12px.
