Scheme 01
Brand Blue + Aqua

Recommended — closest to guidelines

The most brand-faithful adaptation. Brand Blue header, Aqua Blue accents, Cloud background warmed to a soft glacier tone. This is what the guidelines intended, just translated to a modern app surface. Every color is straight from the spec.

Scheme 02
Night Sky + Mango

Premium and bold. Night Sky header, warm Cloud background, Mango for rewards and points. This is the confident, elevated read — the kind of palette you'd see from a health tech company that wants to signal maturity. Warmer than Scheme 1 thanks to the Mango running through it.

Night Sky header — darker, more premium. Reads "established health tech" rather than "startup." The depth creates better contrast for white text than Brand Blue alone.
Mango as the warm engine — habits, active minutes, points all run through the gold-amber family. Makes the app feel warm and rewarding against the cool Night Sky anchor.

Scheme 03
Aqua + Green Energy

The most consumer-wellness option. Aqua Blue leads as primary — it's already the brand's most distinctive and energetic color. Spring Green and Lime handle activity metrics. No color-block header (Withings influence meets MHC palette). This is the direction if you want to feel fresh and modern while staying fully on-brand.
