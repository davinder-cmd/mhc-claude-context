V5 Design Strategy Rewards dashboard is the hero with massive 1,240 points display, 4-tab type switching, gold progress bar, and 12-day streak badge. Sections below reframe health actions as earning opportunities: "Earn more" (not "What to do next"), "Quick earns" (daily cards), "Points from activity" (habits). Gold (#F9A825) accent only on rewards elements. Challenges positioned as earning mechanics. All other UI stays neutral grey/monochrome.
