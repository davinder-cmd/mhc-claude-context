# Stakeholder Outreach Messages

> Working better together conversations. Use as-is or edit to match your voice. Goal is to open a real conversation, not extract a questionnaire.

---

## Darcy Loeb — Head of Product
**Format:** 1:1 meeting request
**Timing:** Now — she's newly in the role, this is the natural moment.

> Hey Darcy — congrats again on the new role. I'd love to grab 30 minutes to make sure design is set up to support what you're building toward. A few things I'd want to cover: what success looks like for you this year, where you want design to show up differently, and anything you need from me that you're not getting. Happy to work around your schedule.

## Alex Young — VP of Product

## Questions to Listen For

Regardless of what they say explicitly, pay attention to:
- What they mention first — that's what's most on their mind
- What they hesitate on — that's where the tension is
- What they ask *you* in return — that tells you what they're missing
